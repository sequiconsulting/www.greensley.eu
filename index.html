About us

We have more than 15 years experience in materials recycling and international trading. By an extensive network of professionals and contracted plants worldwide, we aim to solve the most exciting challenges.

We are a distributed company based in Dublin, with associates and managers working remotely throughout Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas. We use the latest IT technologies for real time information exchange.

We attend regular courses to keep ourselves up-to-date with the latest technologies and scientific developments.

We cover both ends

We work on both sides of the supply chain: we supply raw materials, handle leftovers, and we trade the recycled products

Agency

We represent recycling plants as procurement agents for materials to be recycled

Trade

We source and trade ores, raw materials, recyclables and by-products, worldwide

Research

We find the optimal recycling solutions for bringing new life to industrial leftovers

How we do it

We take direct care of materials qualification and quality assessment.

We handle all documentation to be fully compliant with local and international regulations.

We arrange logistics, finance and provide in-house flow monitoring.

We DO NOT
